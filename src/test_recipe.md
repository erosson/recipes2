Tiffin
======

A delicious, chocolatey treat.

    6 tsp of cocoa powder
    2 tbsp of golden syrup
    1/2 cup of butter
    1/2 cup of sugar
    16oz of digestives
    200g of chocolate

    cover(
        mix(
            heat until bubbling (cocoa powder, golden syrup, butter, sugar),
            crush(digestives)
        ),
        melt(chocolate)
    )
